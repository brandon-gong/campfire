About

Ember is a meditation journal. We know that life and school can be stressful, and we want to support you. Ember contains a an online journal, in which you can be guided with prompts, or just write whatever you think is best. Ember also allows you to analyze how your mood has been changing, and makes suggestions to best support you. We hope that you're able to relax, destress, and continue growing the little Ember within you <3
